‚ÑπÔ∏è How It Works

Real-Time Processing: The indicator analyzes new candle data every 5 seconds and calculates all 6 indicators simultaneously.
Multi-Indicator Confirmation: A signal only triggers when multiple indicators align (e.g., MACD bullish crossover + RSI exit from oversold + price above EMA).
Confidence Scoring: Each confirmed indicator adds to the confidence score. Scores below 60% are filtered out to reduce false signals.
Advance Notifications: Desktop notifications, sound alerts, and in-app popups notify you BEFORE major price movements.
Trend Validation: ADX ensures the trend is strong (>25) before generating signals, avoiding choppy market conditions.
